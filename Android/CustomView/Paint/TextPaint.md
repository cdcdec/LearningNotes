# TextPaint

>   android.text.TextPaint;
 

TextPaint is an extension of Paint that leaves room(空间) for some extra(额外的) data used during text measuring and drawing.


## 构造函数
* TextPaint()
* TextPaint(int flags)
* TextPaint(Paint p)

new TextPaint(Paint.ANTI_ALIAS_FLAG);以抗锯齿的方式创建一个TextPaint.
